Things

I aspire to own as few things as possible.
  • Anticonsumption
    I want to spend my energy on other things than accumulating stuff.

  • Mobility
    I am happy to pack my bag and go live anywhere in the world for an indefinite amount of time. I don't like the astroturfedness of the digital nomad movement, but I do like the freedom from not being tied down.

  • Simplicity
    Having a (good looking) uniform removes choice fatigue.

  • Lanchester's Law
    Fashion and technology rapidly changes. It is less wasteful to upgrade when items are worn out rather than when being obsoleted.

Apart from kitchen utensils, martial arts gear, a bike, a standing desk, monitor and a few other bits and pieces, all my belongings that I need to live my life fit in my 20L backpack.
